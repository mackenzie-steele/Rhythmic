RHYTHMIC

A collection of experiments in building digital products — designed by voice, built by AI, grounded in a background in design and systems. Each project is an exploration: a practice of expanding skills and knowledge as a designer and creator, learning what becomes possible when vision, voice, and the right tools meet. Think of it as a digital journal — not in pursuit of perfection, but in pursuit of learning, curiosity, and play. More at mackenzie-steele.com.
